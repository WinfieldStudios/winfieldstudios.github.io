Mycorrhizae

(“my-kuh-RYE-zee”)

Mycorrhizae is an iOS clicker game with a mushroom theme. Players tap the screen to gather resources and build a vast mycelium network through strategic upgrades.

Design Pillars: Progression, Strategy, Discovery & Education

Core Mechanics:

  • Tap to collect spores
  • Spend spores to gain water
  • Trade water for candy
  • Consume candy to boost spores per tap and gain passive spores

Everything is subject to change or adjustment!
