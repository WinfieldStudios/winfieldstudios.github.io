Galmi

("GAL-me")

A resource-manager clicker game centered around a mysterious rock.

Design Pillars: Idle/Clicker, Resource Management, Engine Builder, Passive World Building

Core Mechanics:

  • Click Rock to Gain Rocks
  • Trade Down Resources for More Valuable Resources
  • Upgrade Rock and Buying Workers to Increase Rock Production
  • Upgrade Pickaxe to Access New Resources and Progression

Mycorrhizae

("my-kuh-RYE-zee")

Mycorrhizae is an iOS clicker game with a mushroom theme. Players tap the screen to gather resources and build a vast mycelium network through strategic upgrades.

Design Pillars: Progression, Strategy, Discovery & Education

Core Mechanics:

  • Tap to collect spores
  • Spend spores to gain water
  • Trade water for candy
  • Consume candy to boost spores per tap and gain passive spores

Mycorrhizae's development is only for iOS.
