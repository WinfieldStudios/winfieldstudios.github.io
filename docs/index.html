Mission

We started small. Just a kid named Kyle coding games alone at 14. Over a decade later, He’s still here, still building, still obsessed with the feeling of a great idea coming to life.

Winfield Studios LLC was founded to bring that feeling to others. This studio is about growth, in the strange, organic, mycelial kind of way. We value game feel, immersion, and meaning in even the smallest interactions. Every sound, every tap, every visual choice matters. That’s the magic.

Whether you’re here to play our games or are just curious, thanks for being a part of the beginning.

Projects

Mycorrhizae

(“my-kuh-RYE-zee”)

Mycorrhizae is a clicker game with a mushroom theme. Players tap the screen to gather resources and build a vast mycelium network through strategic upgrades.

Design Pillars: Progression, Strategy, Discovery & Education

Core Mechanics:

  • Tap to collect spores
  • Spend spores to gain water
  • Trade water for candy
  • Consume candy to boost spores per tap and gain passive spores

Features:

  • Autosaves
  • GenAI Art
  • Music & Sound Design
  • Underground Shop
